STAFF VALUES

*We provide a staff environment that fosters positive character and accountability and that values individuality by treating all members Of the school staff with dignity and respect .

*We help to insure and orderly atmosphere conducive to learning , in the classroom and on campus , by communicating school rules to Students and parents and by modeling and enforcing these rules .

*We honor classroom instructional time and minimize classroom interruptions in order to maximize student learning .

*Through consistent, high expectations for teaching and learning , we will hold students responsible for thinking , creating , and Problem solving and encourage students to recognized and transfer the aesthetics in each discipline .

*We will design and implement standards- based lessons and assessments based on curriculum maps and CBSC Standards.

*We will be committed to the committed to the social, emotional, and academic development of each student by recognizing and nurturing their individual potential and talent .

STUDENT'S VALUAES

CITIZENSHIP

*We will follow school rules and be responsible for our action .

*We will be active , respectful , and supportive citizens in our school .

*Will be keep our campus clean , and will not litter or vandalize our school.

BEING RULE MODELS AND ADVOCATES

*We will value education and clearly communicate its importance to our children .

*We will insure that our children attend school everyday and take their learning responsibilities seriously .

* We will encourage our children attend to set goals and do their best to that they can reach their full potential .

*We will model and promote a work ethic that value attendance , punctuality , preparedness and participation in and Outside the classroom .

*We will ensure that our children arrive at school on time and with the necessary supplies .

*We will support that staff at HOWARD , and will remind our children that teachers and administrators are looking out for student's best Interests .

BEING INFORMED

*We will stay informed of school activities by reading school diary and accessing electronic communications .

*We will continually monitor our children’ s progress by regularly checking their grades on school Loop and /or contacting their teachers .

BEING INVOLVED

*We will attend parent –oriented school function such and parents meeting and open house .

*We will encourage our children to be involved in activities in and outside the classroom , and will we support these activities By volunteering time and / or providing financial support for such activities .

PERSONAL CHARACTERISTICS

*We will engage in positive activities that promote healthy relationship while preventing conflicts , fights , and drama .

* We will not engage in bullying , harassment , profanity , or any other activities that harm others physically and mentally .

*We will promote a safe environment by dressing appropriately and ensuring the safety of our surroundings .

*We will work to manage our stress, to get enough sleep , and to lead healthy and lifestyles .

*We will utilize all available resources hear at HOWARD , including teachers , counselors , psychologists, and administrators, in order to resolve conflicts and maintain our personal well being .

*We will respect ourselves , our peers, and our school by not cheating or plagiarizing .

* We will use technology responsibly .

PARENTS VALUES

BEING GOODS PARENTS

*We will mentor our children, be attentive to their needs and be actively involved in all aspects of their life .

*We will provide a home environment that is conducive to personal achievement and success .

*We will place the physical , social , mental and academic development of our children as a top priority , and will therefore Continually be available to our children for help and support .

*We will modal responsibility , respect and tolerance at home and encourage our to do the same .

*We will maintain open lines of communication with our children so that we may know their successes and struggles at school, Their interests and their friends .

*We will communicate expectation , provide structure , and set appropriate limits for our children .

*We will discuss with our children or necessary issues regarding their personal health, safety and well - being , and will do What is required to help them in these areas .

*We will strive to not be discouraged by the difficulties of raising children, and will remember the importance and value of being involve in our children’s lives .
